## Canonical construct decision

The public instrument uses six substantive dimensions: Counsel, Judgment, Command, Correction, Repair, and Reform.

Telemetry Integrity is cross-cutting and has two visible components: institutional-record integrity and assessment-packet integrity.

## Stable-contract lineage rule

A future `1.0.0` release must preserve the complete `0.x` lineage, identify every breaking change, keep historical contracts immutable, publish the clean-room audit record, and state the exact research maturity supported at release time.
