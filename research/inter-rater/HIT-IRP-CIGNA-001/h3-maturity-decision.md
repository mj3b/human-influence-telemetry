# H3 and Maturity Decision

**Decision date:** 2026-07-18  
**Protocol:** `HIT-IRP-CIGNA-001`  
**Decision owner:** Mark Julius Banasihan

## Evidence

Two eligible independent scorers completed the locked Cigna PxDx exercise under the frozen `0.1.0` scorer contract. Both final JSON transcriptions were verified by their respective scorers before comparison.

The deterministic pre-adjudication comparison produced:

- seven exact agreements across seven compared items;
- an exact-agreement proportion of `1.0000`;
- zero critical disagreements;
- no disagreement rows;
- an undefined supplementary Cohen's kappa because both scorers used the same category across all six substantive dimensions.

The declared advancement gate required at least six exact agreements and zero critical disagreements.

## H3 decision

H3 is supported for this bounded exercise.

> Two independent reviewers reached the predeclared agreement threshold using the published materials and one frozen public-record packet.

The supported claim is narrower than general inter-rater reliability. The exercise contains two scorers, one retrospective case, one evidence packet, and seven compared items.

## Maturity decision

HIT advances from Maturity Level 1, Defined, to Maturity Level 2, Applicable.

The Level 2 entry criterion requires a stranger to apply the method and the locked human exercise to pass its declared independence and agreement rules. The completed exercise satisfies that criterion.
