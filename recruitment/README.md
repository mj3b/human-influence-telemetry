# HIT Independent Scorer Recruitment Package

This directory supports recruitment for the locked human inter-rater protocol `HIT-IRP-CIGNA-001`.

The recruitment package does not modify the protocol, lower the two-scorer requirement, or change the advancement threshold. It exists to make participation bounded, auditable, and easier for a solo maintainer to coordinate.

## Package contents

- `invitation.md`: concise outreach message
- `eligibility-screen.md`: pre-scoring eligibility and conflict screen
- `scorer-instructions.md`: operational instructions after eligibility is confirmed

## Required conditions

A scorer must:

1. be someone other than the HIT author;
2. have made no material contribution to HIT, the Cigna case assessment, or the locked protocol;
3. be able to evaluate governance, assurance, legal, policy, or socio-technical evidence with professional competence;
4. disclose relevant conflicts;
5. not have inspected the author's Cigna findings before locking the submission;
6. work without consulting the author or the other scorer.

The human exercise remains pending until two eligible independent submissions are received.
