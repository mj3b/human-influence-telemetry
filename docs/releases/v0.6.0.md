# Human Influence Telemetry v0.6.0

## Independent inter-rater result

Release `0.6.0` publishes the first locked human inter-rater result for Human Influence Telemetry.

Two eligible independent scorers applied the archived `0.1.0` specification, handbook, and scorer schema to the frozen Cigna PxDx packet `HIT-IR-CIGNA-PXDX-001` under protocol `HIT-IRP-CIGNA-001`.

The pre-adjudication comparison produced seven exact agreements across seven compared items, an exact-agreement proportion of `1.0000`, and zero critical disagreements. The declared advancement gate required at least six exact agreements and zero critical disagreements.

The supplementary substantive-dimension Cohen's kappa is undefined because both scorers assigned the same category across all six substantive dimensions. The data contain no category variance from which to estimate chance-corrected agreement.

## Research decision

Claim H3 is supported for this bounded exercise. HIT advances from Maturity Level 1, Defined, to Maturity Level 2, Applicable.

The result establishes reproducible application for two scorers, one retrospective public-record case, one frozen packet, and seven compared items. It does not establish general inter-rater reliability, cross-domain validity, causal validity, legal correctness, field effectiveness, certification, or independent institutional adoption.
