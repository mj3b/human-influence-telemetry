# DEPS to DEAS Transition Note

The working **Decision Evidence Portability Specification (DEPS)** is being renamed **Decision Evidence Applicability Specification (DEAS)** in the Governed Decision Intelligence repository.

The change corrects the implied claim that governance evidence, controls, findings, or legal conclusions can be transported across regimes without loss of meaning or force.

DEAS asks four bounded questions:

1. Is a defined decision-evidence artifact relevant to a specified governance requirement?
2. What assurance claim may that artifact support?
3. What additional local evidence is required?
4. Where does apparent equivalence fail?

The rename does not change HIT's role. HIT evaluates whether human authority retained practical force. DEAS evaluates what HIT or GDI evidence may support under a specified regime.
